About Us

The Service You’ve Come To Depend On

Our beginning was a lot like many small businesses that started out. It started with one person and a vision. Our founder,Timothy G. Schneider started TGS in 1985 in the back room of his father-in-law’s fruit packing shed. After only six months, and a lot of hard work and success, Tim incorporated. Within the next year he bought the property across the street, where we are today.

In 1993 after graduating from business school, Tim’s son Peter started working at TGS full-time. After only six months of working together, Tim sat Peter down to ask what he thought of the business and what he wanted…the answer is what TGS is today.

Together, Tim and Peter have grown TGS into a well-known and highly respected drayage company in California…and now Nevada. TGS offers an array of drayage services to meet your demands. The operations includes international drayage, domestic intermodal drayage and LCL services.

Since our inception, our focus has not wavered. TGS wants to be the best company for its customers, employees and partners. Not the biggest, the BEST! “The Service You’ve Come To Depend On” is a slogan that Peter sat down and thought long and hard about for several months back in 1994.  It is how TGS thinks about every customer, employee and associate. TGS wants everyone to be happy…not just the customer, but also their employees and industry partners that help make TGS the best.

From humble beginnings to a fully integrated, tech savvy corporation, we are steadfast in maintaining the best, delivering the best, so you always look your best!

International Drayage

Out of northern Nevada, our Reno/Sparks office manages freight from all the international terminals and rail ramps out of the port of Oakland. We run a mix of company trucks and subhaulers.TGS has purchased brand new trucks for our company fleet in Nevada. Lightweight, high horsepower trucks to haul heavy loads and take on Donner Pass with ease. TGS is also incorporating our new chassis fleet into this market. TGS runs about the newest chassis fleet in the industry. All chassis are 2017 and newer, with new chassis coming into the fleet every six months. We have an array of chassis for many types of loads, specifically for heavy 20’ and 40’ loads with upwards of 46,000 lbs. cargo weight.
